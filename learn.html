Pronunciation Scoring & Settings

Our speech analysis system evaluates your audio input and scores it. You can completely customize the evaluation criteria via the Settings menu in your user profile.

Accuracy Threshold

❌ Below Threshold (e.g. < 70%) — Highlighted in Red

✓ Above Threshold (e.g. ≥ 70%) — Highlighted in Green

Adjust your passing mark (from 50% to 90%) in Settings to match your current skill level. Words exceeding this value are marked as successfully mastered.

Speech Synthesis Speed

❌ Standard Playback (1.0x) — Fast voice guides can obscure phonetic details

✓ Custom Slow Speed (0.7x - 0.9x) — Allows clear articulation of complex syllables

Control the playback rate of the audio guide synthesizer to break down and hear the phonetic transitions of native speakers.

Real-time Visual Feedback

❌ Static Interface — Hard to verify if microphone is actively capturing audio

✓ Animated Waveform — Visualizer waves bounce dynamically with audio volume

Keep track of input capture status. The interactive voice visualizer wave responds in real-time when clicking "Listen" or "Speak".

Pronunciation Tips

1

Listen Actively

Pay close attention to native speakers and try to mimic their pronunciation exactly.

2

Record Yourself

Recording and listening to your own pronunciation helps identify areas for improvement.

3

Practice Daily

Consistent daily practice, even for just 10 minutes, is more effective than occasional long sessions.

4

Focus on Sounds

Master individual sounds before moving on to complex words and sentences.
